Who We Are

More than
a club — a community

The Nippon Students Association (NSA) at UNSW is a student-run society dedicated to celebrating Japanese culture, language, and the connections that bring people together. Whether you're a complete beginner or a seasoned Japan enthusiast — you belong here.

We run events year-round, from intimate language exchanges to large-scale matsuri festivals, always with the same mission: to build a welcoming space for everyone passionate about Japan.
